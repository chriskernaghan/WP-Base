CATAGEN is a technology innovation company engineering solutions to clean, decarbonise and secure the air, fuel and energy systems the world depends on.

Built on deep expertise in thermodynamics, catalysis and e-reactor technology, CATAGEN challenges conventional infrastructure models with modular, scalable systems designed for real-world impact.

At the heart of CATAGEN is the e-reactor — a flexible reactor technology platform that enables precise thermal and chemical processes across multiple industries. First proven in emissions testing, the platform is now being adapted for sustainable fuels, defence energy resilience and industrial applications.

Frequently asked

Questions, answered plainly.

If you don't see what you're looking for, get in touch with our team — we'll come back to you the same week.

What is CATAGEN and what does the company do?
CATAGEN is a clean-tech company developing technologies that accelerate the transition to net zero. We deliver Green Emissions Testing for OEMs, sustainable fuel production through ClimaHtech Green Flight, hydrogen compression through ClimaHtech Compress, and biohydrogen and e-fuel technologies across the ClimaHtech platform.
What industries do CATAGEN work with?
Automotive OEMs and Tier-1 suppliers, aerospace and aviation, defence and government, energy producers, and industrial decarbonisation programmes.
What is Green Emissions Testing at CATAGEN?
Accelerated catalyst ageing and emissions testing for OEMs, powered by CATAGEN's founding OMEGA e-Reactor — delivering faster, cleaner and more reliable vehicle development.
What is ClimaHtech and how is it related to CATAGEN?
ClimaHtech is CATAGEN's platform for next-generation net zero technologies. It includes ClimaHtech Green Flight (SAF), ClimaHtech Compress (hydrogen compression), biohydrogen, e-fuel and other clean energy technologies.
Does CATAGEN offer support with emissions compliance?
Yes. We work with OEMs and regulators globally on emissions compliance pathways, supported by our testing infrastructure and engineering team.
What is CATAGEN's OMEGA Technology?
OMEGA is CATAGEN's founding e-Reactor technology — the platform on which Green Emissions Testing was built, and which underpins our wider e-reactor portfolio.
Where is CATAGEN located?
Headquartered in Belfast, Northern Ireland — Channel Commercial Park, Queens Road, BT3 9DT.
