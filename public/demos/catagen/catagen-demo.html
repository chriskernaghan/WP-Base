Electrically driven reactor technology for emissions testing.

CATAGEN's OMEGA Technology is a proprietary, electrically driven reactor platform that reproduces world catalyst and aftertreatment conditions without a full engine or vehicle. Frequently asked

Green Emissions Testing, explained.

If you don't see what you're looking for, get in touch with our team — we'll come back to you the same week.

What is Green Emissions Testing at CATAGEN?
Green Emissions Testing at CATAGEN is a suite of advanced emissions testing, catalyst ageing and characterisation services delivered using electrically driven reactor technology — including highly variable, engine-representative ageing without the need for a full engine or vehicle.
How is CATAGEN's Green Emissions Testing different from traditional engine-based testing?
It replaces slow, costly engine programmes with electrically driven reactors that reproduce catalyst and aftertreatment conditions precisely — delivering faster, more repeatable results at a fraction of the cost and emissions.
What is catalyst ageing and why is it important for emissions compliance?
Catalyst ageing simulates the degradation a catalyst experiences over its service life, so its end-of-life emissions performance can be verified against regulatory limits before deployment.
Can CATAGEN support alternative fuels and new powertrain technologies?
Yes. The reactor platform adapts to alternative fuels, hybrid systems and advanced catalyst materials, reproducing the new degradation mechanisms these introduce.
Is CATAGEN Green Emissions Testing suitable for regulatory submissions?
Results are engine-representative and regulation-relevant, and CATAGEN works with OEMs and regulators globally on compliance pathways supported by recognised accreditation.
